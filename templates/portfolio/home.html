About Dr. Vigneshwaran

Nephrology training grounded in high-volume kidney care and evidence-based medicine.

{{ doctor.name }} is a nephrologist trained through high-volume academic and tertiary-care centers in Tamil Nadu and Kerala. His work includes chronic kidney disease care, dialysis supervision, acute kidney injury management, transplant-related nephrology, and renal care for medically complex hospital patients.

His clinical interests include kidney disease, proteinuria, glomerular disorders, resistant hypertension, recurrent urine infection in kidney patients, and metabolic issues that can contribute to kidney stone recurrence or declining renal function.
